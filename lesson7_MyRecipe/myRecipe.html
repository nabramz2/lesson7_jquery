Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 package (1/4 ounce) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1-1/4 cups warm water (110° to 115°)
  • 1/4 cup canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 3 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 medium green pepper, diced
  • 2 cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese

Instructions

1. In large bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in water.

2. Let stand for 5 minutes.

3. Add oil and salt. Stir in flour, a cup at a time, until a soft dough forms.

4. Turn onto floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, about 2-3 minutes.

5. Place in a greased bowl, turning once to grease the top.

6. Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 45 minutes.

7. Meanwhile, cook beef and onion over medium heat until no longer pink; drain.

8. Punch down dough; divide in half.

9. Press each into a greased 12-in. pizza pan.

10. Combine the tomato sauce, oregano and basil; spread over each crust.

11. Top with beef mixture, green pepper and cheese.

12. Bake at 400° for 25-30 minutes or until crust is lightly browned.
